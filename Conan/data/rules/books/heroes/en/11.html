Elevation and Line of Sight As a general rule, in the case of an abrupt change in elevation, only the areas directly overhanging share a line of sight with the areas below. If two areas are of different elevation levels, for a line of sight to exist between them, the imaginary line cannot cross any area with an elevation level equal to or higher than the higher level of the two areas. When line of sight is in doubt, players should use their best judgment to determine if line of sight exists, or refer to the online Conan Companion (https://companion.the-overlord.com/Conan/). Hindering The Guard action is not affected by hindering (see page 17–18). When a Hero is attacked, after the attack power is determined, the Hero may defend against the attack. The Hero chooses whether or not to perform a Guard action. To perform a Guard action, the Hero assigns one or more gems from their Reserve zone to their Guard space. The Hero may then choose one of their Asset cards that has a Guard bonus to defend with, such as a weapon or shield. If the Hero chooses to defend with one of their Asset cards, they are considered to be parrying. Otherwise, the Hero is considered to be dodging. A Hero can choose only a shield when parrying a Ranged Attack, area attack, or a spell. Without a shield, these attacks can only be dodged. For each gem assigned, the Hero rolls one die of the type indicated on their Guard space 1 , plus the dice indicated on the chosen Weapon card, and their Armor if they have one (see page 12). After resolving any rerolls, the Hero determines the defense power by adding up the number of successes shown on the dice.
