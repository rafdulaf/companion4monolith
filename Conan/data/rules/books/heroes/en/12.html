Armor Asset cards like leather armors and other protections are what essentially grant Armor. It’s a “minimum default” defense that is a free action (not requiring any spending of energy) and which a Hero benefits from automatically for each attack they suffer when they have such an equipment. Whether a Hero uses a Guard action or not (see page 11), they still benefit from the Armor of one of their Asset cards with the Armor bonus when calculating their defense power.
