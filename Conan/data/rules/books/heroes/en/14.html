Hindering
The Reroll action is not affected by hindering (See page 17–18).
A hero can perform Reroll actions after
rolling dice but before finalizing the results
of the roll.
To perform a Reroll, the hero assigns one or
more gems from their Reserve zone to their
Reroll space.
The hero then rerolls that many dice.
A hero can perform the Reroll action multiple times and may
reroll the same dice or different dice each time.
