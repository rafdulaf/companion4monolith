Hindering The Reroll action is not affected by hindering (see page 1718). A Hero can perform Reroll actions after rolling dice but before finalizing the results of the roll. To perform a Reroll, the Hero assigns one or more gems from their Reserve zone to their Reroll space. The Hero then rerolls that many dice. A Hero can perform the Reroll action multiple times and may reroll the same dice or different dice each time.
