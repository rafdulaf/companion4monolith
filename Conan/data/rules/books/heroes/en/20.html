Game Turn Example H adrathus, Shevatas, and Conan have infiltrated a Pict camp in search of a princess they know to be held captive in one of the huts. The Overlord has just completed their turn, so it is now the Heroes’ turn. Conan and Shevatas are both in the same area and still relatively fresh, so they choose to be Aggressive this turn; they each move two gems from their Fatigue zones to their Reserve zones. Hadrathus, exhausted from casting two spells, chooses the Cautious stance; he moves five gems from his Fatigue zone to his Reserve zone. Conan takes the lead to enter the hut nearest to him. This costs him 2 movement points: 1 for crossing the area’s border + 1 for the flap at the hut’s entrance, which are covered by his base movement value. Inside, he encounters three Picts and a chest, but no princess.
