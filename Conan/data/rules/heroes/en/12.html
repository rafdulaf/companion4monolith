Armor
Asset cards like leather armors and other
protections are what essentially grant
Armor. It’s a “minimum default” defense
that does not require any spending of
energy and which a hero benefits from
automatically for each attack they suffer
when they have such an equipment.
Whether a hero uses a Guard action or not
(see page 11), they
still benefit from the Armor of one of their equipment cards
with the Armor bonus when calculating their defense power.
However, they must declare whether or not they Guard before
using their Armor.
