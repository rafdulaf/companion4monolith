When a character moves or performs an action in an area in
which the number of enemy characters is
equal to or higher
than the total number of friendly characters, that character
may be affected by hindering.
Hindering Movement
If the number of enemy characters in a character’s area is
equal to or higher than the number of friendly characters in
that area, that character must spend extra movement points
to move out of that area.
Important:
The Overlord’s characters movement are also
affected by hindering.
