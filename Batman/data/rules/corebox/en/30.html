can activate 0, 1 or 2 tiles each turn.

UPKEEP VILLAIN
All energy cubes that are neither in the fatigue zone nor
in the reserve zone must be moved to the fatigue zone.
The villain recovers a number of energy cubes corre-
sponding to their recovery value, given by the mission.
TRIGGER THE START
OF THE VILLAIN’S TURN EFFECTS
Some eﬀects are linked to the start of the villain’s
turn. If at least one of the trigger conditions is ful-
ﬁlled, the corresponding eﬀects take place in the
order chosen by the villain.
