The Missions Booklet indicates the num- ber of Heroes required for your chosen Mission, as well as the speciﬁc Heroes that you must choose from. You must play with the exact number of Heroes listed for the Mission, even if it means a player takes control of more than one Hero. First, choose one of the available Heroes from each set, as indicated by the Mission. For example, in ‘To Sink a City’, the Hero players must choose a total of 3 Heroes to use.
