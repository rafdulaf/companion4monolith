BATMOBILE’S RULES
The Batmobile can only be used in certain missions.
In the game, the Batmobile is considered a character,
except it is played with a Batmobile board rather than
a character tile.
The Batmobile board is assembled during mission
setup. Diﬀerent components are allocated to each of
the component spaces to create a unique version for
that mission.
